The Problem

Patients forget. Staff chase.
Reschedules get messy.

Clinics lose time and money because patients forget appointments, front-desk staff manually follow up by call, reschedules turn into a mess, and enquiries get lost across WhatsApp and phone calls.

  • 01

    Patients forget appointments

    No automated reminder means patients simply forget — leading to empty slots, lost revenue, and a clinic that can't plan its day.

  • 02

    Staff waste time on manual follow-ups

    Receptionists spend hours calling patients to confirm or chase reschedules — time they could spend on the people already in the clinic.

  • 03

    Reschedules have no process

    When a patient wants to reschedule, it becomes a back-and-forth that falls through the cracks — especially on busy days with multiple staff involved.

  • 04

    Enquiries get lost

    New patient enquiries arrive over WhatsApp, phone, and Instagram — and with no system in place, many simply never get followed up.

The Solution

A WhatsApp patient follow-up system,
set up for you.

Intutron automatically reminds patients, asks them to confirm or reschedule, notifies the front desk, and helps recover missed appointments — without your staff doing it by hand.

Automatic appointment reminders

Patients get a WhatsApp message 24 hours before their appointment — no call needed from your staff.

Confirm or reschedule with one reply

Patients reply 1 to confirm or 2 to reschedule. The system handles both branches automatically.

Front desk notified in real time

When a patient confirms or asks to reschedule, your front desk gets a clean notification on Google Sheets or WhatsApp — no inbox-checking needed.

Missed appointment recovery

If a patient misses their slot, a follow-up message goes out automatically — giving you a second chance to bring them back.

FAQ

Questions clinic owners ask.

Is my patients' data safe? ↓
Yes. Patient data stays inside your own systems — your WhatsApp Business account, your Google Sheet, your booking software. Intutron sets up the automation flows and connects them, but we do not store patient names, phone numbers, or appointment details on our servers. We are conscious of India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act and build our systems accordingly. We do not sell, share, or misuse any data from your clinic.
How long does setup take? ↓
14 days or less from the date you share your clinic's details. The first week is setup and testing, the second week is staff training and a live trial run. You won't go live until you're happy with how it works.
What are the real ongoing costs — no surprises? ↓
You pay Intutron's monthly retainer (₹3,999–₹9,999 depending on your plan). Separately, WhatsApp Business API access and Meta's per-message fees are billed directly by Meta or the BSP (Business Solution Provider) you use — Intutron does not mark these up or take a cut. Most clinics spend ₹500–₹1,500/month on Meta fees depending on message volume.
What if something breaks or a flow stops working? ↓
Monthly support is included in all plans. If a flow breaks, a template gets rejected by Meta, or something behaves unexpectedly, you message me and I fix it. For Beta and Growth plans this is covered in the retainer. Most issues are resolved within 24 hours.
Do I need to learn any software or tool? ↓
No. You don't need to touch Wati, AiSensy, WABA, or any platform directly. Intutron handles all of that. Your staff only needs to know: check the Google Sheet for daily confirmations and reschedules. That's it. If your staff can use WhatsApp, they're already qualified.
What if I want to stop after a few months? ↓
The Beta Clinic Partner plan has no long-term lock-in. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. You keep your WhatsApp Business account and any templates that were approved by Meta — those belong to you. Growth and Premium plans may have a 3-month minimum given the higher setup investment; this will be clearly stated in your agreement before you sign.
